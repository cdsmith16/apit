Meet Annafi

The Flip Side is the brainchild of Annafi Wahed. A liberal utterly baffled by the 2016 election, she quit her corporate job in NYC and spent four months campaigning in New Hampshire for the Democratic Party. In that time, she learned just how entrenched the liberal and conservative media bubbles are.

In the months following the election as talk of fake news and media bias became commonplace, Annafi asked herself a simple question: how can the average person escape their media bubble without disrupting their daily routine? Few people have the time or desire to read 20 op­eds a day...
